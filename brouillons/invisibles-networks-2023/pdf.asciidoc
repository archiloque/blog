== Invisible networks

link:https://twitter.com/ctrlcreep/status/1638937123372802057[Invisible networks] is a writing jam organized by link:https://twitter.com/ctrlcreep/[ctrlcreep]. The topic is "`Invent a weird/magical/deeply sinister social network every day`", and each day provides a prompt (and three bonus prompts in case you're not inspired by the default ones).

Thanks to them for it.
